Frequently Asked Questions:

Q. What is the UBER challenge?
A. As a part of this challenge, you will be given a set of tasks to promote Uber in the city and on successful completion of the challenge, candidates will be selected for next round based on performance.

Q. What is the duration of the internship?
A. Minimum 3 months

Q. Are there different shifts?
A. No night shifts

Q. As college ambassadors what would be the perks?
A. Certificate, Coupons and other perks based on candidate’s performance

Q. What are the skills that any candidate applying must have?
A. Communication Skills, Presentation Skills, Operational Skills

Q. Is candidate supposed to carry any document other than resume?
A. Round 1 doesn’t require any document.

Q. Should they carry stationary if there is pen paper test?
A. It’s a surprise task which will be revealed on the day of event.

Q. Is the task online or should a candidate be present for the offline task?
A. It’s an offline task. Candidates must be present at the assigned college on the day of event.
