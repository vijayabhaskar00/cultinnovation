ABOUT CONTEST:

CULT INNOVATION YATRA 2020 is intended to be an ambitious initiative by CULT and supported by TSIC. It involves 120 enthusiastic and creative participants from all walks of life coming together on a journey of 4000 kilometres across Telangana to ideate, innovate and initiate start ups and businesses. From problem identification in local villages to meeting local innovators to attending workshops that will get them ready on the basics of launching a successful start up, the participants and many others involved in the journey will get a unique, first hand encounter of the Telangana innovation ecosystem
Finding Arijit In Colleges is about giving a platform to all singers to showcase their talent. The contest consists of 3 rounds.
1. Audition: All the participants from all over the city will compete to move onto Semi-Finals at their nearest venue.
2. Semi-Final: Judges of the audition would select the candidates for this round. The contestants would have a compete in the battleground.
3. Grand Finale: Students selected from semi-finals would compete to win the chance of performing with Arijit Singh. Rewards & Perks For The Participants
1. The winner gets a chance to meet Arijit Singh.
2. Top 5 will get a chance to click a picture with Arijit Singh and access to the VIP lounge.
3. All the participants would receive passes to the concert.
4. Many exciting goodies for the on-spot contest. Select your zone where you want to participate for the audition round Venue Zones for the Auditions: Medchal | Secunderabad | Uppal | Ghatkesar | Nadergul | Moinabad |Kukatpally
